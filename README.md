# Ohio TVSF Round Reports — Analysis (Rounds 31–44)

A 49-slide HTML presentation analyzing the Ohio Third Frontier Technology Validation and Start-Up Fund (TVSF) across Rounds 31–44 (Jan 2023 – Apr 2026).

**[View the live presentation →](https://vanderpoolteacher.github.io/ohio-tvsf-analysis/)**

## What's inside

- **`index.html`** — self-contained 49-slide presentation. No build step, no external dependencies. Open in any modern browser. Navigate with arrow keys (← →), Space, or click.
- **`Ohio_TVSF_Reports/`** — the 14 source PDFs from Ohio Department of Development, downloaded and saved locally (Rounds 31–44).

## Coverage

The presentation is structured in four parts:

1. **Program-level analysis** (slides 1–16) — Funding by round, approval-rate trends, sector mix, status of the ~78 funded Phase 2 startups, geography by region, NW Ohio drill-down, observations.
2. **Featured companies — marquee notables** (slides 17–25) — Lyceum AI, Enspire DBS, Afference, CARTx Therapeutics, Pavaka Wound Care, NeoIndicate, BrandRank.AI, Auxilium Health.
3. **Northwest Ohio companies** (slides 26–32) — Every applicant from NW Ohio across 14 rounds, funded and rejected.
4. **Comparables for NWO strategy** (slides 33–47) — 14 entities exemplifying federal-lab IP channels, ag biotech, advanced materials, water tech, and ESP/incubator models, each with a specific lesson for NW Ohio's ecosystem.

Slides 48–49 close with the bottom-line takeaway and an appendix.

## Source data

All round-report PDFs were originally published by the Ohio Department of Development at:

> https://development.ohio.gov/business/third-frontier-and-technology/technology-validation-start-up-fund

Status assessments were compiled from public web sources (company websites, LinkedIn, Crunchbase, Ohio Secretary of State business search, news mentions) as of May 2026.
